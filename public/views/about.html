Where do all these board games come from?

All the data comes from boardgamegeek.com.

How many board games may I found here?

The quiz uses only the most owned games according to boardgamegeek.com (i. e. games owned by at least 2000 users).
There is around 1000 games for this quiz.

How the score is calculated?

Each correct answer grants a specific amount of points.
The remaining seconds after a correct answer grant additional points.
Multiple correct answers in a row grant additional points (up to 5 good answers).
